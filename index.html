Most of our planet is covered by the oceans.

Also most of our oxygen is produced from the ocean. A healthy planet means healthy oceans and these ocean ecosystems are under attack. Here you can find designs of your favoirte sharks and octopus. Not only can you get some great designs, but you can help keep the oceans clean. All proceeds will be donated to ocean conservations charites.

Sharks are the ocean's immune system.

They help clean up the ocean thru feeding on dead marine life, and they keep the food chain on track. Sharks are under threat of extintion and without our sharks the ocean ecosystem will collapse.

Octopuses are very fasinating animals.

They are know for their intelligence and color changing abilites. They are also really fun to draw.
